Apply for unemployment insurance

Unemployment insurance (UI) pays part of your income after your employer lays you off or reduces your hours.

How to apply

Applying online is the fastest way to file for unemployment insurance. You can apply during the times below.

Hours

Days Available times
Sunday 5 a.m. – 8:30 p.m.
Monday 4 a.m. – 10 p.m.
Tuesday – Friday 2 a.m. – 10 p.m.
Saturday 2 a.m. – 8 p.m.

If you mail or fax your application, expect processing to take up to 10 days.

Print and fill out an application form:

  • If you worked in California: English | Spanish
  • If you worked in California and another state: English | Spanish
  • If you served in the military: English | Spanish
