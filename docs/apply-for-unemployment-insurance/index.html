Apply for unemployment insurance

Unemployment insurance (UI) pays part of your income after your employer lays you off or reduces your hours.

Who can apply

To get UI, you must be:

  • Totally or partly unemployed
  • Unemployed not because of anything you did
  • Physically able to work
  • Available and willing to accept work immediately
  • Actively looking for work

You also must have made enough money in the last 18 months to get UI benefits. Find out if you meet the limit and what your UI payments would be by calculating your benefits.
