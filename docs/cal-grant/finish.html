Finish applying for a Cal Grant

You’ve applied for a Cal Grant as long as:

  • You've applied for financial aid by March 2
  • You or your school has shared your GPA with us by March 2

Thanks for applying for a Cal Grant!

What happens next

  • You can check WebGrants 4 Students to see if you qualify 7–10 business days after you apply.
  • Once you select a school, log into WebGrants 4 Students to confirm your award and get your money.

If you're a high school senior

We'll send you a letter in the mail to let you know if you qualify for a Cal Grant.

If you're a high school graduate or in college

We'll send you an email to let you know if you qualify for a Cal Grant.
