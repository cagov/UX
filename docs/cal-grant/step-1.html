Apply for a Cal Grant: Step 1

Deadline to apply: Monday, March 2, 2020

Apply for financial aid

There are two options to apply for financial aid.

  • Apply for the California Dream Act Application (CADAA) if you:
  • are undocumented
  • have Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA) — valid or expired
  • have a U Visa
  • have Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
  • or are eligible for AB540
Apply for CADAA
  • Apply for the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) if you:
  • have U.S. Citizenship
  • are a Permanent Resident
  • do not have U.S. Citizenship, but you are eligible for federal student aid
  • or have a T Visa
Apply for FAFSA
