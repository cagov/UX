Apply for a discounted phone service (California LifeLine)

Follow these six steps to apply for California LifeLine, a discounted phone service.

  • 1 Check if you qualify ShowHide

    • Check if you qualify
  • 2 Choose an eligible phone company ShowHide
    • Find an eligible phone company
  • 3 Call your phone company ShowHide

    Ask your phone company about their California LifeLine program for a phone service discount. They should review your eligibility and start your application process.

    You can ask to get voice assitance through the process or letters with braille, if you'd like.

    ShowHide
  • 4 Read our pink letter in the mail ShowHide

    In three weeks, you should get a pink letter in the mail. Open it to find your PIN number. You’ll need your PIN to complete your application.

    ShowHide
  • 5 Apply online or by mail ShowHide

    Have your PIN number with you. Then you can:

    • Apply online
    or

    Apply by mail. Simply fill out and mail back the form inside your pink letter.

  • 6 Read our decision in the mail ShowHide

    We’ll send you a letter to let you know if you’re approved for a discounted phone service.

    If you're approved, you’ll start getting a monthly discount from your phone company automatically.
