Apply for a discounted phone service

California LifeLine lowers the cost of your monthly phone bill. This discount is for households that:

  • Get public assistance
  • Or have an income below a certain amount

Steps to apply for California LifeLine

If you need help, contact us.

  • ShowHide

    Ask your phone company about their California LifeLine program for a phone service discount. They should tell you if you can get a discount and start your application.

    You can ask to get personal help through the rest of the application or letters with braille, if you need it.

    ShowHide

    In three weeks, you should get a pink letter in the mail. Open it to find your PIN number. You’ll need your PIN to complete your application.

    ShowHide
  • ShowHide

    Have your PIN number with you. Then you can:

    • Apply online
      • or
    • Apply by mail. Simply fill out and mail back the form inside your pink letter.
  • ShowHide

    We’ll send you a letter to let you know if you’re approved for a discounted phone service.

    If you're approved, you’ll start getting a monthly discount from your phone company automatically.
