Apply for a discounted phone service

California LifeLine lowers the cost of your monthly bill for your cell or landline phone. This discount is for households that:

  • Get public assistance
  • Or have an income below a certain amount

Steps to apply for California LifeLine

If you need help, contact us.

  • ShowHide

    • Check if you can get a discount
  • ShowHide
    Find a new phone company that offers a California LifeLine discount. Or check if your existing phone company offers a discount.
    • Find a phone company
  • ShowHide

    Ask your phone company to start your application for the California LifeLine program. They should tell you if you can get a discount and start the application process for you. Then, we'll send you a letter in the mail.

    You can ask to get personal help through the rest of the application or letters with braille, if you need it.

    ShowHide
  • ShowHide

    In three weeks, you should get a pink letter in the mail. Open it to find your PIN number. You’ll need your PIN to complete your application.

    If you contacted your phone company but still haven’t gotten your application form in the mail, please contact us.

    ShowHide
  • ShowHide

    Have your PIN number with you. Then you can:

    • Apply online
      • or
    • Apply by mail. Simply fill out and mail back the form inside your pink letter.
  • ShowHide

    We’ll send you a letter to let you know if you’re approved for a discounted phone service.

    If you're approved, you’ll start getting a monthly discount from your phone company automatically.

  • ShowHide

    One year from your start date, we'll send you another pink letter in the mail asking you to renew your discount. Then, you can use it to renew by mail or online.
