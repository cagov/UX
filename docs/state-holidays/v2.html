How holiday credits work

  • Employees can spend their personal and holiday credits on the days they choose between July 1 and June 30.
  • Every year, most employees get one personal holiday.
  • When a holiday is on a Saturday, most employees get one holiday credit (eight hours of leave).
  • When a holiday is on a Sunday, most employees have the day off the following Monday.
