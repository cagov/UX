Apply for disability insurance benefits

Steps to get disability insurance

You could possibly get these short-term payments if you can’t work because you’re:

  • sick
  • injured
  • pregnant
  • 1 Check if you’re eligible ShowHide
    • Find out if you’re eligible for disability insurance benefits
    • Learn if your employer has a voluntary plan
    • Learn about disability insurance for self-employed workers
  • 2 File your claim ShowHide

    File online

    or

    File by mail

  • 3 Ask your doctor to certify your disability ShowHide

    A doctor has to certify your medical condition. They must submit a form within 49 days of the first day you couldn’t work because of your disability.

    Your doctor can certify your disability online or by mail.

    ShowHide
  • 4 Read our response in the mail ShowHide

    Within 14 days, we will mail you a letter to let you know if you qualify for disability benefits.

    If you do qualify:

    • You can get paid eight days after the start of your claim.
    • You can choose to get paid through a debit card or checks in the mail. (Checks take 7 to 10 days to deliver.)

    If you don’t qualify:

    • You can tell us you disagree with the decision.
      • In your letter, you’ll also get an Appeal Form. You can fill out this form and send it back to us.
      • You have 30 days from the day we sent your notice to respond with your Appeal Form.
    ShowHide
  • 5 Submit a form to keep getting paid ShowHide

    To keep your benefits, you have to let us know you still have a disability. To do that, you must return one of the forms below. Please note: If you don’t, your payments will stop.

    If you get automatic payments:

    • We will send you a form after 10 weeks.
    • The form is called Disability Claim Continuing Certification.
    • You must return this form to continue your benefits.

    If you don’t get automatic payments:

    • We will send you a different form every two weeks.
    • The form is called Claim for Continued Disability Benefits.
    • You must return this form to continue your benefits.

    The fastest way to submit these forms is through SDI Online.
